This page is currently just for testing - signing up here will *not* sign you up as a party member. Come back soon though, and it will!

Being a member of Something New is completely free, though we ask that you set up a regular donation when you join.

Everything we do is open to all; there are no exclusive member benefits (though you will need to join if you want to stand for election). Instead it's about making a statement, and helping to build a better future.

We don't mind if you are a member of another party - in fact, we encourage collaboration! Your other party might have a problem with it though; check their rules.
